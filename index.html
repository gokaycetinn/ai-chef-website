About AI Chef - Intelligent Cooking Assistant

AI Chef is a comprehensive mobile application that transforms your cooking experience using modern artificial intelligence technologies. Built with React Native and Expo, our app provides intelligent kitchen solutions that make cooking easier, more creative, and more efficient.

Smart Ingredient System

Select from 40+ categorized ingredients across 8 categories. Our visual ingredient selection with custom icons makes finding what you need quick and intuitive.

AI-Powered Recipe Generation

Google Gemini AI integration creates personalized recipes based on your selected ingredients. Get detailed instructions, cooking times, and nutritional information.

Smart Food Recognition

Snap a photo of any dish and our AI will identify it instantly. Get automatic recipe suggestions for dishes you see and want to recreate.

Comprehensive Recipe Database

Access 100+ pre-prepared recipes across multiple categories including breakfast, main courses, desserts, and beverages. All recipes include detailed nutritional information, cooking times, step-by-step instructions, and high-quality food photography to guide your cooking journey.
