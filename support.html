Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about AI Chef.

How does AI Chef generate recipes?

AI Chef uses advanced machine learning algorithms powered by Google Gemini AI to analyze your selected ingredients and generate personalized recipes. The AI considers ingredient combinations, cooking techniques, nutritional balance, and flavor profiles to create unique recipes tailored to your preferences.

How accurate is the ingredient recognition feature?

Our ingredient recognition feature uses advanced computer vision technology and is generally very accurate for common ingredients. However, we recommend always verifying the identified ingredients before proceeding with recipe generation. The system continuously learns and improves its accuracy over time.

Can I use AI Chef without an internet connection?

AI Chef requires an internet connection for core features like recipe generation and ingredient recognition, as these rely on cloud-based AI services. However, you can view previously saved recipes and access your favorites offline. We're working on expanding offline capabilities in future updates.

Is my personal data safe with AI Chef?

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. All personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We only collect data necessary to provide our services and never sell your information to third parties. For detailed information, please read our Privacy Policy.

How do I save and organize my favorite recipes?

To save a recipe, simply tap the heart icon when viewing any recipe. You can access all your saved recipes in the "Favorites" section. You can also create custom collections and organize recipes by meal type, cuisine, or dietary preferences through your profile settings.

Can AI Chef accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies?

Absolutely! AI Chef can generate recipes based on various dietary preferences including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, keto, and more. You can set your dietary restrictions in your profile, and the AI will consider these when generating recipes. However, always double-check ingredients if you have severe allergies.

What languages does AI Chef support?

Currently, AI Chef supports English and Turkish, with plans to add more languages in future updates. The app automatically detects your device language and provides the interface and recipes in the appropriate language.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

We welcome your feedback! You can report bugs or suggest features by emailing us at aichef.smart@gmail.com with "Bug Report" or "Feature Request" in the subject line. Please include as much detail as possible, including your device type and app version.

Is AI Chef free to use?

AI Chef offers core features for free, including recipe generation and ingredient recognition with daily usage limits. We also plan to offer premium features with unlimited usage, advanced nutritional analysis, and exclusive recipe collections in future updates.

How do I sync my data across multiple devices?

Create an account with your email address to automatically sync your recipes, preferences, and favorites across all your devices. Simply log in with the same account on any device to access your personalized AI Chef experience.
